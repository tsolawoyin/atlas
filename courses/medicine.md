# Medicine and Surgery

### Introduction

Medicine and surgery are two closely related but distinct fields within healthcare, focusing on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of illnesses and conditions affecting the human body.

#### Definition of Medicine

Medicine is the science and practice of diagnosing, treating, and preventing diseases and injuries through non-invasive methods such as medications, lifestyle changes, and other therapeutic approaches.

#### Definition of Surgery

Surgery is a branch of medicine that involves physically altering body tissues through operative techniques to treat diseases, injuries, or deformities.
